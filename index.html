About Me

Hi, I'm Anastasiia.

Photography for me is more than just pressing a shutter button. It's the art of seeing beauty in details, sincerity in emotions, and capturing those "unposed" moments that become the most precious.

For over 5 years, I have been helping my clients see their true selves against the backdrop of beautiful Prague and across Europe. My goal is to make you feel light and confident during the shoot.
